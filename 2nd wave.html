    • 2nd wave of covid 19 in India

    Symptoms:


    • Fever
    • Dry cough
    • Fatigue
    • Loss of taste or smell,
    • Nasal congestion,
    • Conjunctivitis (also known as red eyes)
    • Sore throat,
    • Headache,
    • Muscle or joint pain,
    • Different types of skin rash,
    • Nausea or vomiting,
    • Diarrhea,
    • Chills or dizziness(Weakness).

    Covid-19 patients was lower in the second wave. Higher proportion of patients in
    the younger age group of less than 20 years and those between 20-39 years got
    hospitalized during the second wave. Overall, around 70% of the admitted patients were
    40 years and above in both waves of the pandemic. The mortality among hospitalized
    patients increased by 3.1% in the second wave .
